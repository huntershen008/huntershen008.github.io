Top 5 Features of AuroraPrime Create That Will Make You a More Efficient Medical Writer

November 11, 2024 · 3 min read

Medical writing is a demanding field that requires a high level of accuracy, clarity, and efficiency. AuroraPrime, a GenAI-powered medical authoring platform, can help you meet these demands and become a more efficient medical writer. Here are the top five features of AuroraPrime that will transform your writing process:

1. AI-Driven First Draft Automation​

One of the most time-consuming aspects of medical writing is creating the first draft. AuroraPrime uses generative AI to automate this process, generating up to 80% complete drafts in minutes. This significantly reduces the time you spend on initial drafting, allowing you to focus on more complex tasks like analysis and interpretation.

For example, for Clinical Study Reports (CSRs), AuroraPrime can reduce first draft time by 90% and overall production time by 45%. Imagine the time savings!

2. Effortless Document Template Configuration​

Medical writing involves creating a wide variety of documents, each with specific requirements. AuroraPrime offers GenAI-powered templates that adapt to different therapeutic areas, disease areas, and study requirements. This eliminates the need to manually format each document, saving you time and ensuring consistency. You can generate new templates from existing documents and store them in a template library for easy access.

3. End-to-End Document Lifecycle Automation​

AuroraPrime seamlessly integrates with your existing document ecosystem, including Microsoft Word, RIM systems, and review platforms. This integration streamlines your workflow, connecting crucial tools and automating tasks throughout the document lifecycle. You can manage updates, track changes, and collaborate with colleagues more efficiently.

4. Tables, Figures, and Listings (TFL) Automation​

TFLs are essential components of many medical documents, but incorporating and updating them can be tedious. AuroraPrime automates TFL management, including:

  • Efficient TFL Insertion: Seamlessly add TFLs to your documents.
  • Automated TFL Updates: Keep TFLs current with minimal effort.
  • TFL Summary Auto-Generation: Generate evidence-based summaries directly from TFL data.

5. Writing Instructions for Enhanced Consistency​

AuroraPrime's writing instructions feature allows you to embed guidelines and standards directly into your documents. These instructions are visible to writers but automatically excluded from the final output, ensuring compliance and consistency.

Benefits include:

  • Streamlined Editing: Clear guidance reduces back-and-forth between writers and editors.
  • Improved Clarity: Documents are free of embedded instructions, resulting in a clean and professional final product.

Beyond Efficiency: Embrace the Future of Medical Writing

AuroraPrime is more than just a tool for efficiency. It represents a shift towards a future where generative AI plays a transformative role in medical writing. By automating repetitive tasks and enhancing accuracy, AuroraPrime empowers medical writers to focus on higher-level contributions, such as data analysis, interpretation, and strategic thinking. As the field of medical writing continues to evolve, tools like AuroraPrime will be essential for staying ahead of the curve and delivering high-quality, impactful work.
