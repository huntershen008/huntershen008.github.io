AlphaLife Sciences Announces Availability of AuroraPrime on Microsoft AppSource

November 13, 2024 · 3 min read

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

New York, U.S.A - November 13, 2024 - AlphaLife Sciences, a global leader in Generative AI (GenAI)-driven clinical development solutions, today announced the availability of its AuroraPrime Create Microsoft Word add-in and Software as a Service (SAAS) solution on Microsoft AppSource, an online cloud marketplace providing tailored line-of-business solutions.

AlphaLife Sciences is partnered with innovators like Microsoft, Google, Nvidia, and Johnson & Johnson Innovation - JLABS and is dedicated to reshaping drug development and expediting the delivery of life-saving treatments worldwide. AuroraPrime leverages advanced GenAI capabilities like prompt engineering, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and fine-tuning to enhance the entire lifecycle of medical and regulatory documentation. The add-in has proven to be a valuable tool in streamlining the creation of clinical study reports (CSRs), a key component of drug development that requires extensive cross-collaboration and attention to detail.

The AuroraPrime Create add-in was designed to elevate the creation of clinical documentation, tailored specifically for crafting essential clinical documentation like CSRs and study protocols. AuroraPrime enhances efficiency by automating first draft creation, document lifecycle updates, and supporting customizable templates for various therapeutic areas. The platform generates "80% complete" drafts within minutes, significantly accelerating documentation timelines and enabling faster collaborative review.

AuroraPrime Create offers powerful features designed to streamline clinical documentation. Its AI-driven document automation supports a wide range of clinical document types, while large-scale TFL automation enables efficient management and updating of data. Additionally, it provides automatic generation of TFL summaries based on the provided data.

Using AuroraPrime Create brings numerous benefits, including enhanced operational efficiency through streamlined processes and reduced authoring times. It also strengthens regulatory compliance and adherence to guidelines, lowers communication costs among study team members, boosts study transparency, and reduces the number of required reviews.

"We are excited to make AuroraPrime available on Microsoft AppSource, making it easier for pharmaceutical companies to access and implement our innovative GenAI solutions," said Sharon Chen, CEO and founder of AlphaLife Sciences."

"Microsoft welcomes AuroraPrime Create to AppSource, where global customers find thousands of line-of-business partner solutions that work with the Microsoft products they already use," said Giovanni Mezgec, Vice President, Modern Work + Business Applications Field & Partner Marketing, Microsoft Corp. "AppSource and trusted partners like AlphaLife Sciences help customers do more with less by increasing efficiency, buying confidently, and spending smarter."

The AuroraPrime Create add-in is easy to install and use:

  • Install from Microsoft AppSource
  • Install from Microsoft Word: Users can manually install the add-in from the Office Add-Ins Store by searching for "AuroraPrime Create" and clicking "Add."

To learn more about AuroraPrime, please visit https://alphalifesci.com/products/auroraprime-platform.
